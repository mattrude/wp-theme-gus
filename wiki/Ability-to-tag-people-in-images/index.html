This is one of my favorite features of the Gus theme, the ability to tag people you know in your images.

The theme uses Custom Taxonomies for images, one of them is the people taxonomy. The People taxonomy is for who is in the image.

From the Image Page

So now for the fun part. If any user is viewing a image (on the images own image page), they will see a box near the bottom that asks Who is this? (see below).

Entering a name in the above box will look up the name in the people taxonomy and display a list of possible people already in the databases, as you type. All you need to do is select the correct person and hit Enter.

From the Admin Page

Next from the WordPress Admin, go to the Community Tags link on the sidebar.

Once your in the admin, you will see a screen like this if there are any waiting tags to be approved.

All you need to do is choose 'Approve', then save the page once your done. This will add the image to that person's 'people' taxonomy. Users may then see a list of all pages for the person by going to 'http://example.com/people/sam/'.
