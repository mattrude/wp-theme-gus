Gus is a simple theme with a lot of features. It was built for a single user to use on their site. Please follow the [[installation|Installing]] guide for new sites.

Features

Below is a more in depth look at each of the listed features.

  • [[Photo Gallery Support]]
  • [[Custom Fonts|Fonts]]
  • [[Gallery index page]]
  • [[EXIF Support for images]]
  • [[Ability to tag people in images]]
  • [[Table of Content]] generator
  • [[Custom taxonomy]] (people, place, event)
  • [[Custom people gallery page]]
  • [[5 widget footer section]]
  • [[Threaded comments]]
