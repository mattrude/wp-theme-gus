Gus has built in Gallery post_type support on it's index pages. This allows Gus to display it's gallery items in a different format then it's other content types.

Depending on how your site is built, depends on how you display your galleries. In this example, we will assume Galleries are placed in the 'Gallery' category in WordPress.

Since Gus shows a static front page, Gallery post_type items are only shown on index pages.
